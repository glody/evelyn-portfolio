关于我 / About

我专注于文化创意产品设计、视觉传达与包装设计，希望将地域故事、传统符号与当代审美结合，创造出兼具温度、表达力与实用性的作品。 I focus on cultural and creative product design, visual communication, and packaging concepts. My work brings together local stories, traditional symbols, and contemporary aesthetics to create designs that feel meaningful, expressive, and approachable.
